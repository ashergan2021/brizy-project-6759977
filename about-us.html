Our Vision

InvestEdu came to life when its founders realised that youths and young working adults do not receive sufficient financial education in school and therefore may not have sufficient knowledge to make the best financial decisions when they start their careers. Individuals are often confused, overwhelmed, and sceptical of advice when faced with advice from online videos, financial advisers, or family and friends. Hence, InvestEdu strives to provide finance-related content, along with the experience and community learners can gain by participating in our courses.
